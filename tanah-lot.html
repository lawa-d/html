Tanah Lot Temple

Explore the beauty of this iconic Balinese sea temple.

About Tanah Lot Temple

Tanah Lot is one of Bali's most iconic temples, located on a rock formation in the sea. It's renowned for its stunning sunsets and is considered a spiritual place for the Balinese people. The temple is dedicated to the Balinese sea gods and offers an impressive view of the Indian Ocean.

Things to Do at Tanah Lot Temple

Aside from visiting the temple, tourists can explore the surrounding area, enjoy the scenic views, and witness traditional Balinese dances and ceremonies. Don't forget to take a walk around the temple during low tide to get closer to the rock formation!
