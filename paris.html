About Paris

Paris is the capital city of France and one of the most visited cities in the world. Known for its history, art, architecture, and culture, Paris is often called the "City of Light." The city is famous for its museums like the Louvre, iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, and romantic ambiance. It’s a global center for fashion, food, and culture.

Things to Do in Paris

Paris offers something for everyone, from exploring world-renowned museums like the Louvre to walking along the Seine River and through the charming streets of Montmartre. Visitors can climb the Eiffel Tower, enjoy a boat cruise along the Seine, or indulge in delicious French cuisine at local cafés.
